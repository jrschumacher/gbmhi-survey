Social Capital and Networking Survey

In preparation for the second gathering of the Greater Boston Muslim Health Initiative (www.gbmhi.org) on October 18, 2014, we would like to document the resources and relationships of local organizations that currently promote well-being among the region’s diverse Muslim population. We are asking key individuals to fill out a short survey. The preliminary results of this mapping exercise will be presented and discussed at the conference, in order to strengthen and expand a collaborative network. We would like for your organizations to be included on the map and in the discussion. Please take 10-15 minutes to fill out this online survey.

The community map and organizational network will be revised during and after the GBMHI conference. We hope to publish a map on our website, with some brief general comments about the types of relationships that exist. Our hope is that this information will be useful to all the organizations listed. The information will also be analyzed by a group of students in Dr. Lance Laird’s medical anthropology course, “Islamic Medicines and Healing” at Boston University during the fall of 2014. Students will then conduct interviews with some individuals from model organizations. You are under no obligation to participate in any part of this survey and interview process.
